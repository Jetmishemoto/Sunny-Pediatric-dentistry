Your New City Pediatric Dentist

Your family is in good hands with Dr. Blooth! His passion for healthy smiles began during childhood as he grew up watching and learning from both his father and grandfather, who were both dentists. Dr. Blooth is skilled, knowledgeable, and strives to create a community of healthy smiles through dentistry that is compassionately delivered. His hope is that through focusing on patient comfort and quality care, he can form lasting relationships with families. When the whole family is involved in the patient's dental care, strong and healthy habits are formed. Dr. Blooth loves working with children and getting to see his patients thrive with happy, healthy smiles.
